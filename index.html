How it works

Three steps. No ISBN required.
No formatting headaches.

If you can attach a file to an email, you can publish on OpenShelf.

01

Upload your book

Drop in your PDF and a cover image. Add a title, a short description, and pick your genre. That's the entire process. No formatting tools, no technical specifications, no approval queue.

02

Set your terms

One decision: sell it or share it free. Set your own price, change it anytime, or offer it at no cost. OpenShelf never locks you in or takes your rights.

03

Readers find you

Your book appears on genre pages, curated shelves, and search results. Readers browse paid and free titles side by side — discovering voices the big platforms would never surface.

About OpenShelf

Built for writers.
Not for gatekeepers.

Publishing your work shouldn't require a technical degree. We built OpenShelf because the existing platforms are either too complex, too expensive, or take too much. There's a better way.

OpenShelf is an independent marketplace giving every writer — from first-timers sharing a poem to seasoned authors building a catalog — the same simple tools to reach readers on their own terms. No ISBN. No formatting software. No waiting weeks for approval.

⚡

Live in minutes

Upload, describe, publish. The entire process takes under 10 minutes for most authors.

💰

10% flat fee — nothing hidden

We take 10% on paid sales only. Free books cost nothing. Ever. No monthly fees.

🔓

You own your work

Your rights, your files, your readers. OpenShelf is a platform, not a publisher.

📖

PDF-first, always

No conversion tools. No formatting checkers. If you have a PDF, you're ready to publish.
